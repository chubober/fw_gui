This website is dedicated to collecting language corpora. The corpora are used for studies in syntax, especially in differential object marking.


We invite you to upload your own data or take a look at available corpora.

The available corpora are Moksha and Khanty. These are texts collected during the joint filed work of the Higher School of Economics and Moscow State University teams (2013-2015, and 2018-2021 correspondingly). They were annotated for different factors regulating the choice of object marking in these languages.

This website is supported by RFBR and GACR, project number 20-512-26004 "The morphology pf agreement" (Исследование выполнено при финансовой поддержке РФФИ и ЧНФ в рамках научного проекта № 20-512-26004).
